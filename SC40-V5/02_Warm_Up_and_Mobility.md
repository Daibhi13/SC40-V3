# Warm-Up & Mobility for Sprinting

A proper warm-up prepares your body for explosive sprinting and reduces injury risk.

## Dynamic Warm-Up Routine
- Light jog (3-5 minutes)
- Leg swings (front-to-back, side-to-side)
- High knees
- Butt kicks
- Arm circles

## Mobility Drills
- Hip flexor stretches
- Ankle mobility exercises
- Spinal rotations
