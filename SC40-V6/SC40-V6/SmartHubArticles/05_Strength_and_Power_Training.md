# Strength & Power Training for Sprinters

Building strength and power is essential for improving sprint performance.

## Key Lifts
- Squats (back, front)
- Deadlifts
- Power cleans
- Lunges
- Step-ups

## Plyometrics
- Box jumps
- Depth jumps
- Medicine ball throws

## Tips
- Focus on form and explosiveness
- Allow full recovery between sets
